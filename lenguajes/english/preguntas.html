Frequently Asked Questions

What is a section?

Internal Section: Internal sections are a specific part or division of content within a page. Additionally, they can contain text, images, videos, links, and other multimedia elements, and are often used to present different topics, categories, or featured aspects of a website. Therefore, sections are usually visually delineated by design elements such as borders or distinctive backgrounds, which help differentiate them from each other.

What is a external section?

External Section: On a website, external sections are portions of content that exist outside the initial body and are inserted from other pages or external websites apart from the main one. These sections provide additional content beyond what is offered by the 'internal' section. For instance, this very section is external, as you access it by clicking from an internal one, enabling you to gather more information about it. It can also include functionalities sourced externally, such as updated news, social media feeds, or subscription forms, without overloading the main page.

What is a SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO):" Refers to the techniques used to enhance the visibility and positioning of a website in search engine results. This involves optimizing various aspects of the site, such as content, structure, and technical elements, with the goal of increasing its relevance and attracting more organic traffic from search engines. SEO aims to achieve higher visibility in the top positions of relevant search results, which can lead to increased visits and business opportunities for the website.

What is a self-managed?

Self-ManagedIt's the ability of owners or administrators to manage and update their content independently, without relying on third parties. This involves making changes to the design, adding or editing content, managing users, and performing other tasks related to the website without the need for advanced technical knowledge

¿Cuales son los diseños webs?

El diseño web influye en varios aspectos de una pagina y puede tener un impacto significativo en la experiencia del usuario y en el éxito general de un proyecto en línea.

* El diseño web básico: se caracteriza por su simplicidad y funcionalidad, con una estructura de navegación clara y un enfoque en proporcionar información de manera directa y concisa.

* El diseño web retro: se inspira en estilos y elementos visuales de épocas pasadas, utilizando colores, tipografías y gráficos vintage para evocar una sensación de nostalgia y darle al sitio web una estética distintiva.

* El diseño web minimalista: se enfoca en la simplicidad y elimina elementos innecesarios, utilizando colores neutros, mucho espacio en blanco y una tipografía clara. El objetivo es resaltar el contenido principal y proporcionar una experiencia de usuario limpia y sin distracciones.

*El diseño web moderno: se basa en las últimas tendencias y estándares de diseño, utilizando diseños asimétricos, colores audaces, tipografía creativa y efectos visuales interactivos. Este estilo busca ser innovador y se adapta a las preferencias y tecnologías actuales.

Web hosting: It is a service that enables you to have your website online by storing all the necessary files and resources on specialized servers.
